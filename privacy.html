Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

TripCo respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account or use TripCo, we may collect your email address, basic profile information (such as name if provided via Apple/Google login), and the travel itineraries and spots you choose to save.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information strictly to provide, maintain, and improve our services, including syncing your saved spots across devices and personalizing your travel recommendations.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share anonymous, aggregated travel data to improve our AI models, but this will never be linked back to your identity.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time by contacting our support team or deleting your account from the Profile settings.
